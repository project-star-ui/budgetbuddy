Meet Your Liver!

The liver is your body's largest internal organ and sits on the right side of your belly, tucked under your ribs.

It's about the size of a football and works super hard every day to keep you healthy!

Cool Liver Facts!

Detox Hero

Your liver cleans your blood by removing harmful substances like medicines and chemicals!

Energy Factory

It stores sugar (glucose) and releases it when your body needs extra energy!

Digestion Helper

The liver makes bile that helps your intestines break down yummy food!

Germ Fighter

It helps fight infections by removing bacteria from your blood!

The Liver's Digestive Job

Bile Production

The liver makes about 1 liter of bile every day! This special liquid helps break down fats in your food.

1. Food Arrives

Food with fat enters your digestive system

2. Bile Gets Made

Liver creates bile to break down the fat

3. Nutrients Absorbed

Now your body can use the healthy fats

The liver (the red part) works with the digestive system!
